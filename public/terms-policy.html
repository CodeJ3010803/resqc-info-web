Terms of Use and Privacy Policy

This page explains acceptable platform use, user responsibilities, and how ResQC handles emergency-related data.

Terms of Use

1. Intended Use

ResQC is for genuine emergency and public safety coordination only.

2. User Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate information when submitting incident reports.
  • Do not submit prank, false, or malicious emergency requests.
  • Use respectful communication in all chats and calls.

3. Account and Access

Access may be suspended for misuse, security risks, or policy violations.

4. Emergency Limitations

While designed for rapid response support, ResQC does not guarantee dispatch time, responder availability, or outcome.

Fake bookings, prank alerts, and intentional false reporting are strictly prohibited and may be referred to proper authorities under applicable Philippine laws and local ordinances.

Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

  • Profile and contact details you provide.
  • Location and incident report data (including media attachments).
  • Communication logs required for emergency coordination.

2. Why We Use Data

  • To route incidents to the correct responder teams.
  • To maintain emergency status updates and audit trails.
  • To improve platform reliability and response workflows.

3. Data Sharing

Data is shared only with authorized emergency personnel, partner agencies, and service providers when necessary for emergency response operations.

4. Security

ResQC uses role-based access controls, secure cloud storage, and encrypted channels where applicable.
